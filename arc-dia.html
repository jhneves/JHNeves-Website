Arc Dia

Arc

On Arc desktop I worked on browser features that sat close to both the user surface and the browser stack. The work spanned Swift UI, Chromium-facing integration, and the debugging needed to keep the product fast and stable.

A lot of the job was making new behavior feel native to the browser rather than layered on top of it.

Dia

On Dia I worked on early product work around contextual AI in the browser. That meant connecting tab context, prompt behavior, and native UI into something that still felt clear and fast.

The interesting part was not just the prompt layer. It was making the whole interaction hold together across product design, client behavior, and browser architecture.

Role

My role cut across product surfaces and supporting systems: shipping UI in Swift, working through Chromium integration points, and improving instrumentation when regressions or performance issues showed up.

It is some of the clearest product-systems work in the portfolio: browser UX, prompts, and client performance all needing to move together.
