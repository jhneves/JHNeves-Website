Arc & Dia

Arc Dia

At The Browser Company I worked across Arc desktop and Dia, building browser features that sat right at the boundary between product experience and browser architecture. The work covered native UI, AI-native interaction design, and the systems work needed to make those features feel fast and coherent inside a browser.

The stack spanned Swift, Chromium-facing integration, C++, prompt behavior, and performance instrumentation, depending on where the product boundary landed. A lot of the job was making new behavior feel native to the browser instead of layered awkwardly on top of it.
