The vision

Arc and Dia sat at the intersection of browser UX, applied AI, and low-level systems work.

The work was not just about adding AI to a browser. It was about making intelligence feel native to the product, while preserving speed, polish, and trust in the interaction model.

That meant coordinating prompt behavior, browser context, interface design, and the underlying Swift and Chromium bridge so the product felt cohesive rather than stitched together.
