Arc & Dia

Browser work where AI features, interaction design, and systems engineering had to feel like one product.

The Browser Company - Staff Software Engineer - 2024-2026

Overview

The work across Arc and Dia sat right at the boundary between product behavior and lower-level systems. The challenge was not just to add AI capabilities, but to make them feel native to the browser instead of bolted on top of it.

That meant connecting prompt behavior, browser context, Swift UI, and Chromium internals into something that felt cohesive, fast, and trustworthy.

What I Focused On

I worked on AI-driven browser features, polished product surfaces in Swift, architectural bridges into Chromium, and the instrumentation needed to understand where product performance was holding up or falling apart.

It is some of the clearest product-systems work in the portfolio: UX, prompts, performance, and platform engineering all needing to move together.
