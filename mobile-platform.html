Salesforce Mobile

A long-running platform modernization effort across architecture, automation, hybrid systems, and product innovation.

Salesforce - Staff Software Engineer - 2014-2022

Overview

This was multi-year platform work inside a flagship mobile app that still had to keep shipping while the underlying systems were being improved.

The scope went beyond architecture. It included instrumentation, CI/CD, hybrid systems, automation, developer leverage, and several innovation efforts that eventually became patents.

What I Focused On

I worked on framework refactoring, system adoption across teams, developer productivity, and the kind of engineering work that makes a large product easier to evolve without freezing delivery.

It was also where a lot of my product-systems instincts were formed: modernize the foundation, improve the tools around it, and create room for better product work to happen on top.
