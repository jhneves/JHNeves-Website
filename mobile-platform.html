At Salesforce I spent years on the mobile platform layer inside a very large product, improving the foundations while many teams still had to keep shipping on top of them. The work was less about any one feature and more about making the app easier to evolve, release, and reason about over time.

The stack and responsibilities spanned iOS architecture, framework refactors, hybrid systems, CI/CD, automation, release tooling, and developer infrastructure. Several patents came out of that period, but the stronger signal is the kind of engineering it required: sequencing deep platform change without slowing the rest of the product down.
