Key outcome

Built a cross-platform game with custom mechanics and physics-driven interaction.

The vision

The project was an exploration of feel: movement, timing, feedback, and repeatability.

Games make technical decisions visible immediately, because the player feels them before they can name them. That makes them a useful proving ground for systems thinking and iteration.

Save LaLa Again leaned into that, using custom mechanics and physics behavior to make the interaction loop feel distinctive across multiple Apple platforms.

The execution

02

Cross-platform adaptation

03

Fast iteration

Used a compact technical stack to keep tuning loops short and experimentation frequent.
