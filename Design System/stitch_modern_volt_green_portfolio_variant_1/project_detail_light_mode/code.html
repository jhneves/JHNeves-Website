Role

Principal Product Designer

Date

Q3 2023 — Present

Tech Stack
React Python OpenAI Tailwind
Key Achievement

Reduced workflow latency by 45% through predictive UI modeling.

The Vision

Creating a seamless bridge between human intuition and machine intelligence required a total rethink of the traditional canvas-based workflow.

Arc & Dia was born from the frustration of context-switching. Professionals today spend 40% of their time managing tools rather than executing vision. Our goal was to build an environment where the interface disappears, leaving only the creative intent.

By leveraging large language models at the core of the interaction layer, we developed a "Kinetic Interface" that anticipates the next logical step in a user's journey, surfacing tools exactly when they are needed.

The Process

  • 01. Discovery & Synthesis

    We interviewed 50+ creative directors to understand the 'flow state' and where it breaks. The consistent theme was the 'friction of navigation'.

  • 02. Atomic Intelligence

    Unlike traditional design systems, we built an 'Atomic Intelligence' system where every component has an awareness of its context and relationships to other data points.

  • 03. User Testing & Refinement

    Iterating through 14 high-fidelity prototypes, we focused on the haptic feedback and micro-interactions that make the AI feel responsive rather than prescriptive.
