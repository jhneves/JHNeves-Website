upark

An earlier iPhone utility app built around a practical everyday use case.

Independent Builder - iPhone - No Longer Available

Overview

upark came from an earlier phase of my independent work where I was exploring smaller utility products with a very practical user story at the center.

The app is no longer available, but it still matters to the portfolio because it reflects the same habit that shows up in later products: taking a specific problem seriously and trying to solve it with a small, focused app rather than a sprawling feature set.

What I Focused On

The work was straightforward in the best sense. Build the app natively, keep the scope tight, and make the utility immediately understandable on iPhone without unnecessary complexity.

It was one of the projects that reinforced my bias toward shipping something small and useful rather than overbuilding too early.
