Cash App

Money movement and banking work where product delivery had to hold up under real operational risk.

Block - Staff Software Engineer - 2022-2024

Overview

The work covered transfers first and then banking, which meant living in a part of the product where release quality, observability, and user trust mattered as much as the features themselves.

It was not enough for flows to look good or ship quickly. They had to be controllable, understandable, and resilient once they were in the wild.

What I Focused On

I led mobile work across projects that touched both user trust and business performance, using staged rollouts, A/B tests, dashboards, alerting, and runbooks to keep delivery safe.

This was also where I helped push engineering culture toward more automation, visual regression tooling, and stronger release discipline.
