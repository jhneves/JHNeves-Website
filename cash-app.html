Cash App

High-trust mobile work across transfers and banking, where shipping carefully mattered as much as shipping fast.

Block - Staff Software Engineer - 2022-2024

App Store Website

Overview

Cash App put me in the part of mobile engineering where a release is never just a release. It touches money movement, customer trust, operations, and support all at once.

That changed how I approached delivery. Features mattered, but so did rollout design, observability, and making sure the team knew exactly what was happening after launch.

What I Owned

I worked across transfers and banking, owning mobile work that needed both user-facing polish and operational discipline. My responsibility did not stop at implementation. It included staged rollouts, experiment setup, dashboards, alerting, and runbooks.

I also pushed on engineering leverage through better automation, visual regression tooling, and stronger release discipline so launches were easier to reason about before and after they went live.

Visible Outcomes

The result was safer delivery across public transfers and banking surfaces in the iOS app. The work covered both the feature layer and the supporting operational layer that made those launches manageable.

It is the clearest example in the portfolio of product engineering under real operational constraints: not just shipping the flow, but shipping the visibility and control needed to stand behind it.
