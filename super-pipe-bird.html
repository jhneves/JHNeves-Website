Super Pipe Bird

An early App Store game release built around a simple one-tap loop and repeatable challenge.

Independent Builder - iPhone + iPad - Available on the App Store

App Store

Overview

Super Pipe Bird came out of the simplest kind of game question: can one tap, one core mechanic, and the right pacing be enough to make the loop worth repeating?

That simplicity made the bar higher, not lower. With so little surface area, the responsiveness, difficulty curve, and feedback all had to carry much more of the experience.

What I Focused On

The project was built as a compact App Store release for iPhone and iPad, using a lightweight stack so I could iterate on feel quickly and keep the gameplay loop sharp.

It is one of the clearest examples in my own work of how much product quality can come from tuning a very small interaction until it feels right.
