Today Planned

App Store Website

Today Planned is a planning app built to surface the events that actually matter and make them hard to miss. I built it end to end as an independent product, shaping the interaction model, the interface, and the day-to-day experience across iPhone and Apple Watch.

It is built in SwiftUI with SwiftData, widgets, Live Activities, and App Intents, with the same product carried across the phone, watch, and system surfaces. The app is localized across 11 locales, and the full release workflow, including translation, screenshots, and Xcode Cloud delivery, runs automatically.
