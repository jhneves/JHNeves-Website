Key outcome

Shipped a planning product centered on intentional structure rather than noisy productivity tropes.

The vision

The goal was a planning tool that felt calm, opinionated, and useful without becoming heavy.

Many planning apps are either overly rigid or visually noisy. Today Planned was built around the idea that daily planning should reduce cognitive load, not add to it.

That drove both the product structure and the interface tone: fewer decisions, clearer hierarchy, and a more legible sense of what matters today.

The execution

01

Intentional hierarchy

Built the information model around clarity of today’s work rather than feature sprawl.

02

Apple-native design

Used SwiftUI to build an interface that feels quiet, immediate, and platform-appropriate.

03

Cross-device continuity

Extended the same planning philosophy across iPhone and watch without bloating the experience.
