Today Planned

A calmer planning product built around calendar alarms, availability, and a clearer view of the day.

Independent Builder - iPhone + Apple Watch - Available on the App Store

App Store Website

Overview

Today Planned started from a very normal problem: most calendar apps either get noisy or make simple things harder than they need to be. I wanted something that helped me see the day clearly, warn me reliably, and make free time obvious at a glance.

The product is intentionally narrow: reliable alarms, availability sharing, and a cleaner sense of what is next, without turning the day into a dashboard.

What I Owned

I designed and built the product end to end in SwiftUI, including the core planning flows, alarm behavior, availability sharing, and the companion watch experience. The goal was to keep it opinionated and calm instead of endlessly configurable.

It is also a real paid product, so the work includes the less glamorous parts too: shipping, iteration, store presentation, and deciding what not to add. That discipline is a big part of why the product feels the way it does.

Visible Outcomes

Today Planned is live on the App Store as a one-time purchase, not a subscription product. That constraint shaped the product as much as the UI did: it had to be useful quickly, understandable on first use, and worth paying for without relying on bloat.

Its strongest differentiators are practical rather than flashy: reliable calendar alarms, private and offline-friendly behavior, availability sharing, and an Apple Watch companion built around discreet haptics instead of noise.
