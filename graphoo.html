Key outcome

Reached #1 on the App Store in Brazil with an algorithm-heavy product.

The vision

Graphoo combined algorithmic rigor with a mobile interface simple enough to stay approachable.

Projects with deeper computational logic often become difficult to present well. The challenge was to make complex pathfinding and graph reasoning feel comprehensible in a handheld product.

That meant shaping the product around legibility and response, not only around correctness.

The execution

01

Algorithmic core

Used Dijkstra-based logic as a product primitive rather than a hidden implementation detail.

02

Interface clarity

Kept the presentation legible enough that the logic felt empowering rather than opaque.

03

Market response

The app reached #1 in Brazil, showing that dense technical products can still resonate broadly.
