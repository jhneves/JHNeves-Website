Graphoo

Graphoo was an iPhone app that turned graph logic into something people could actually use, which was the interesting part of the project. It went on to reach #1 on the App Store in Brazil, but the stronger signal was making a technically dense idea feel approachable on a phone.

It was built natively in Objective-C around graph algorithms and pathfinding, with the interface doing as much work as the code in keeping the system understandable. It is still one of the clearest examples in my work of turning heavy logic into a consumer product.
