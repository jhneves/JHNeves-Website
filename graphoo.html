Graphoo

An algorithm-heavy iPhone app that reached #1 on the App Store in Brazil.

Independent Builder - iPhone - No Longer Available

Overview

Graphoo was built around a denser algorithmic core than most mobile apps can comfortably expose. The challenge was not only to make the logic work, but to make it legible enough that people could actually use it on a phone.

That balance of correctness and interface clarity is what made the project interesting. The app eventually reached #1 on the Brazilian App Store, which showed that technically unusual products can still find a broader audience when the interaction is clear enough.

What I Focused On

I used Objective-C and graph logic built around Dijkstra-style pathfinding to make the algorithm the actual product, not just an implementation detail.

The interesting work was translating that logic into a product that felt approachable rather than intimidating. That is still one of the best examples of algorithmic thinking turning into a clear user-facing app.
